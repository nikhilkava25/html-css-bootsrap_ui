About Us


PIZZVILLE in 2020 when NK turned a lifelong love of pizza into a small neighborhood spot. What began as a single oven and a handful of recipes grew from one simple goal: make the best pizza using honest ingredients and real technique.

Our Philosophy

We believe exceptional pizza starts at the source. That’s why we work with local farms and dairies to source seasonal produce, artisan cheeses, and responsibly grown herbs. Our dough is made in-house daily and rested for flavor and texture. We bake each pizza to perfection — whether in a wood-fired oven, stone deck oven, or our modern high-heat oven — the result is the same: soulful, satisfying pizza.

Craft & Community

We’re proud to be part of the community. From sponsoring local events to donating pizzas for school fundraisers, giving back is baked into our mission. We also train our team in real pizza craft — from dough stretching to sauce balancing — so every pie reflects skill and care.

What to Expect

A warm welcome, quick service, creative specialty pies, and the classics done right. Dine in, take out, or order online — we bring handcrafted pizza to your table.

Join Us

Stop by, say hi, and grab a slice. Follow us for seasonal specials and local collaborations. Your next favorite pizza is waiting.
